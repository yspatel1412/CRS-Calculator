About the CRS Calculator

What is the Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS)?

The Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) is a points-based system used by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) to assess and score your profile and rank it in the Express Entry pool.

The CRS evaluates factors such as age, education, language skills, and work experience to determine eligibility for Canadian immigration.

How does this calculator work?

This calculator estimates your CRS score based on IRCC's criteria, including:

  • Core human capital factors (age, education, language skills, work experience)
  • Spouse or common-law partner factors (if applicable)
  • Skill transferability (education + work experience)
  • Additional points (job offers, provincial nominations, etc.)
